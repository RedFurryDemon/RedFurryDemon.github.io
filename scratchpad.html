My earliest notes and concept artwork concerning K-143 date back over eight years ago, although I've begun toying around with the idea of a reptile-inhabited world much earlier, perhaps before primary school. There is no particular purpose of this setting - such as a background for stories or the like - or rather, the design itself is the purpose. I suppose I can't properly function without immersing myself in one or another fantasy world every now and then...
